The document is a legal filing proposing a lawsuit by Fabio da Silva Ba1ense against the State of Rio de Janeiro and the Municipality of Rio de Janeiro. Ba1ense is requesting a court order for the provision of necessary medication to treat his neoplasia maligna (cancer) and a waiver of legal fees due to his financial situation. He argues that the denial of these medications violates his constitutional right to public healthcare. The filing also includes medical records and a request for a prompt hearing due to the urgency of his medical condition.
